Game of Skills

Khiladi Zone is considered a “game of skill.” A game of skill can be defined as a game where the skills of the individuals participating in any online e-sports gaming play a dominant role, rather than the mere luck of the individuals. In a game of skill, participants use their knowledge, skills, training, and attention to participate and win. Online e-sports gaming is considered a legal practice worldwide, with certain states being an exception. Online e-sports gaming is entirely based on the concept of a game of skills. The services, contests, and programs related to online e-sports gaming offered by Khiladi Zone are entirely dependent on the skills, knowledge, and attention of the users. This makes online e-sports gaming offered at Khiladi Zone a game of skill and 100% legal.

Alignment with Indian Laws

Games of skill, such as online e-sports gaming, are considered legal throughout India, except in certain states such as Andhra Pradesh, Assam, Orissa, Telangana, Sikkim, Meghalaya, and Nagaland. The Public Gambling Act, 1867 (“PGA, 1867”) is recognized as the primary legal framework governing gambling in India. The PGA defines the act of “public gambling” and the keeping of a common gaming house as criminal and punishable acts in India. However, online fantasy sports gaming is considered an exception under the PGA, wherein the provisions and punishments of the PGA do not apply to games played by users using their skills and knowledge as primary tools.

States with Unclear Laws

There are various states in India where the laws related to games of skill are unclear. In this regard, for websites offering e-sports gaming, it remains unclear whether to offer the services for free or not. The Indian states with no clear regulations regarding the offering of pay-to-play contests include Andhra Pradesh, Assam, Orissa, Telangana, Sikkim, Meghalaya, and Nagaland. In this context, Khiladi Zone does not permit users identified as residents of these states to participate in the pay-to-play contests organized by Khiladi Zone.
